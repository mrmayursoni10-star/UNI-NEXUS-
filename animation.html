Description

The Animation Club is a creative space for students passionate about storytelling, design, and visual art. It brings together individuals interested in animation, motion graphics, and digital creativity to learn, collaborate, and create engaging content. From basic concepts to advanced techniques, the club encourages imagination, skill development, and teamwork through projects, workshops, and creative exploration.
